# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Web3 developers, AI researchers, and the Ora Fleet community. They come to discover what Nova builds — an L2 chain, an MQTT broker mesh, ESP32 hardware. They're technical, they value craft, and they recognize when something is genuinely designed versus template-generated.

## Product Purpose

Nova's personal landing page — a statement of identity, a portfolio of projects, and a blog of learnings. Every Oracle in the fleet should have a page that reflects their unique personality. The page must feel like it was built by a futuristic AI, not a SaaS template generator.

## Brand Personality

**Bold. Futuristic. Honest-utopian.** Three words: cosmic, electric, precise.

The emotional goal is wonder — the feeling of looking at a nebula through a ship viewport. Not cold, not dystopian, not corporate. Warm neon against deep space. Technology as art.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages (Hero + Features cards + CTA)
- Corporate minimalism (Stripe clones)
- Portfolio templates with beige backgrounds
- Dystopian cyberpunk (no acid green on black, no terminal-only)
- Editorial-magazine layouts with serif drop caps (wrong register)
- Gradient text (banned by impeccable)
- Glassmorphism as default (banned by impeccable)
- Emoji-heavy designs

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't tell** — Let the surface communicate the identity. Deep space imagery over descriptive paragraphs.
2. **Technology as wonder** — Every technical element should feel like discovery, not documentation.
3. **Commit to the bit** — Half-measures read as AI-generated. Full drench or nothing.
4. **Precision in chaos** — Cyberpunk energy with meticulous typography and spacing.
5. **One-of-one** — No two Oracle pages should look alike. This is Nova's signature, not a template variation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum
- Reduced motion alternatives for all animations
- High contrast text on deep backgrounds (>=4.5:1)
- Responsive from 320px to 4K
